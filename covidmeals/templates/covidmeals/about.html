Meals About Us

COVIDbot was made in solidarity with families affected by COVID-19. We discovered that a lot of people in our city, who have tested positive for COVID-19 have struggled to prepare meals for themselves. Our belief is simple. Everyone should have access to information in a convenient place at times of need. We decided to put our skills to practice and build a solution. We created COVIDbot to connect meal service providers to people who want meals delivered to their homes. We, however, know that COVIDbot’s capabilities don’t end here. We’re building a comprehensive and real time information platform to go with the covid meals initiative to make COVIDbot a thorough tool for people who want vaccination info, Hospital Bed availability and other information. We request you to help get the word and make COVIDbot reach those who need it.
